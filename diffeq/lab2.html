addendum

here is some mathematica code for how to plot several trajectories on top of the direction field for our example of y'=y: 
dirfield = VectorPlot[{1, y}, {x, -5, 5}, {y, -5, 5}];
Do[p[i] = Plot[i*Exp[x], {x, -5, 5}, PlotRange -> {-5, 5}], {i, -5, 5}];
tab1 = Table[p[i], {i, -5, 5}];
tab2 = Append[ptab, dirfield];
Show[tab2]
